The Cheeseburger - An American Classic

Description

This cheeseburger recipe will leave your belly full and your soul content.

All of your favorites combine for a timeless take on an American classic.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 3 tablespoons dill pickle relish
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 pounds ground beef, 80/20
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 6 slices American cheese
  • 1 pack Hamburger buns
  • Romaine lettuce
  • Sliced tomato
  • Sliced red onion

Steps

  1. SAUCE: In a small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, ketchup, relish, and mustard; set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, combine beef, salt and pepper. Stir and form into 1 inch patties
  3. Heat canola oil in cast iron skillet over medium high heat.
  4. Add patties and cook untii desired doneness.
  5. Top with cheese and sauce; serve immediately.
