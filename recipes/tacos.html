Ground Beef Tacos

Description

A classic weeknight staple - these tacos are filling and are ready to be devoured in less than 30 minutes.

Feel free to customize however you like.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef 80% lean
  • a pinch of salt
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 8 flour or corn tortillas
  • 1/2 cup vegetable or canola oil
  • shredded 4 cheese blend
  • 1/4 cup of water

Steps

  1. Brown the meat. Heat up your skillet over medium heat. Add the ground beef.
  2. Cook the ground beef, breaking it up while it cooks until browned.
  3. Drain excess grease from the skillet. Reduce heat to low.
  4. Season the meat and add the water.
  5. Allow to simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. While meat simmers, wrap tortillas in damp paper towel and microwave for 30 seconds.
  7. Remove tortillas from microwave and fill with seasoned meat and top with cheese.
  8. Enjoy!
